Overnight Oats Recipe
Ingredients:
  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup almond milk
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 tsp honey or maple syrup
  • Fresh fruits for topping
Instructions:
  1. Combine oats, almond milk, chia seeds, and honey in a jar or bowl.
  2. Mix well, cover, and refrigerate overnight.
  3. In the morning, top with fresh fruits and enjoy!
Smoothie Bowl Recipe
Ingredients:
  • 1 frozen banana
  • 1/2 cup frozen berries
  • 1/2 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • Granola and nuts for topping
Instructions:
  1. Blend frozen banana, berries, yogurt, and almond milk until smooth.
  2. Pour into a bowl and top with granola and nuts.
  3. Serve immediately and enjoy!
Avocado Toast Recipe
Ingredients:
  • 1 slice whole-grain bread
  • 1/2 avocado, mashed
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional toppings: egg, tomato slices, or microgreens
Instructions:
  1. Toast the bread to your liking.
  2. Spread the mashed avocado evenly on the toast.
  3. Season with salt and pepper, and add optional toppings as desired.
  4. Serve and enjoy!
Healthy Pancakes Recipe
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp honey
Instructions:
  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl until smooth.
  2. Heat a non-stick skillet and pour batter to form pancakes.
  3. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook the other side.
  4. Serve with fresh fruits and syrup.
Veggie Omelette Recipe
Ingredients:
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup chopped bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup chopped onions
  • 1/4 cup spinach
  • Salt and pepper to taste
Instructions:
  1. Whisk eggs with salt and pepper.
  2. Sauté vegetables in a non-stick pan.
  3. Pour eggs over the vegetables and cook until set.
  4. Fold and serve warm.
Yogurt Parfait Recipe
Ingredients:
  • 1 cup Greek yogurt
  • 1/2 cup granola
  • 1/2 cup mixed berries
  • 1 tbsp honey
Instructions:
  1. Layer yogurt, granola, and berries in a glass.
  2. Drizzle with honey and serve immediately.
