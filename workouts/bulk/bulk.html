Bulk Workout Plan

Here is your six-day plan to bulk up efficiently.

Monday

  • Bench Press: 4 sets of 8 reps
  • Incline Dumbbell Press: 4 sets of 10 reps
  • Push-ups: 3 sets to failure

Tuesday

  • Squats: 4 sets of 8 reps
  • Leg Press: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Calf Raises: 3 sets of 15 reps

Wednesday

  • Deadlift: 4 sets of 6 reps
  • Pull-ups: 3 sets to failure
  • Barbell Rows: 4 sets of 8 reps

Thursday

  • Overhead Press: 4 sets of 8 reps
  • Arnold Press: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Lateral Raises: 3 sets of 12 reps

Friday

  • Barbell Curl: 4 sets of 8 reps
  • Tricep Pushdown: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Dumbbell Curl: 3 sets of 12 reps

Saturday

  • Bench Press: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Dumbbell Flyes: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Incline Push-ups: 3 sets to failure
