Lean Bulk Workout Plan

Here is your six-day lean bulk plan for optimal muscle gain.

Monday

  • Incline Bench Press: 4 sets of 10 reps
  • Dumbbell Press: 4 sets of 8 reps
  • Chest Fly: 3 sets of 12 reps

Tuesday

  • Front Squats: 4 sets of 8 reps
  • Lunges: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Leg Curls: 3 sets of 15 reps

Wednesday

  • Pull-ups: 4 sets to failure
  • Lat Pulldown: 4 sets of 10 reps
  • Dumbbell Rows: 3 sets of 8 reps

Thursday

  • Seated Military Press: 4 sets of 8 reps
  • Dumbbell Lateral Raises: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Cable Front Raises: 3 sets of 15 reps

Friday

  • Barbell Curls: 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Hammer Curls: 3 sets of 12 reps
  • Tricep Dips: 3 sets to failure

Saturday

  • Incline Dumbbell Press: 4 sets of 8 reps
  • Pec Deck Machine: 4 sets of 10 reps
  • Incline Push-ups: 3 sets to failure
